## Target Audience Personas

### Primary (60% of traffic target)
**Persona:** European Leisure Traveler
- **Demographics:** 30-60 years old, German/Dutch/British/Scandinavian, €2000-5000 annual travel budget
- **Mindset:** "I want an authentic, peaceful escape. No all-inclusive resorts or crowds."
- **Search behavior:** "best boutique hotels Kaş," "where to stay Kaş," "quietest beach Turkey"
- **Booking triggers:** Reviews mentioning "local knowledge," "direct owner," "peaceful," "small group"
- **Funnel stage:** Research phase (browsing options), comparison phase (narrowing down)

### Secondary (30%)
**Persona:** Turkish Domestic Traveler
- **Demographics:** 25-55 years old, Istanbul/Ankara/İzmir based, planning weekend/week getaway
- **Mindset:** "I want to support local businesses. Personal service beats OTAs."
- **Search behavior:** Turkish-language queries: "Kaş'ta kalınacak yerler," "Kaş tatil önerileri"
- **Booking triggers:** "Doğrudan rezervasyon," "yerel hizmet," "kişisel ilgi"
- **Funnel stage:** Closer to decision (shorter research, faster booking)

### Tertiary (10%)
**Persona:** Activity-First Adventurer
- **Demographics:** 20-45 years old, diving/hiking enthusiasts, all nationalities
- **Mindset:** "I'm here for scuba/trekking. Where's the best accommodation hub for that?"
- **Search behavior:** "scuba diving Kaş," "Lycian Way hiking near accommodation," "dive sites near hotels"
- **Booking triggers:** "Organized daily dives," "guides available," "gear included"
- **Funnel stage:** Activity-focused, accommodation is secondary

## Search Intent Mapping

### Informational (Research phase) — 60% of content
- **Goal:** Build authority, establish expertise, answer "why Kaş?"
- **Format:** Guides, comparisons, seasonal tips
- **CTA:** "Learn about our properties in [destination]" — soft link to relevant properties
- **Examples:** "Kekova Sunken City," "Lycian Way," "Things to Do in Kaş"

### Commercial (Consideration/Decision phase) — 35% of content
- **Goal:** Position properties as solution, drive direct bookings
- **Format:** "Where to stay," neighborhood guides, property comparisons
- **CTA:** "Book your stay at Kozy Holiday properties in Kaş" — hard link to booking
- **Examples:** "Where to Stay in Kaş," "Çukurbağ Peninsula," "Best Hotels vs Villas"

### Navigational (Brand searches) — 5% of content
- **Goal:** Capture brand searches and direct traffic
- **Format:** Property pages, brand story
- **CTA:** Already built into property pages
- **Examples:** "Kozy Holiday reviews," "BlueNook Suites," "Villa Patara"

## Internal Linking & Content Hub Architecture

### Hub-and-Spoke Model

```
                   KAŞOLITH (Pillar: "Kaş Turkey: Complete Guide")
                              ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑ ↑
          ┌────────────────────┼─┼─┼─┼─┼────────────────────┐
          │                    │ │ │ │ │                    │
      [Where to  [Things to  [Best Time] [Transport]  [Budget]  [Safety/
       Stay in]   Do in]                                        Practicality]
         Kaş       Kaş        to Visit                          FAQs
          │         │           Kaş                             │
          ↓         ↓           │ ↓                              ↓
       [Çukur-   [Kekova]  [Seasonal    [How to get]      [Restaurants]
        bağ]      [Patara]   Guides]     [Transfers]       [Nightlife]
              [Activities: Diving, Hiking, Kayaking]
                    ↓         ↓          ↓
              [Scuba  [Lycian  [Kayaking
               Sites]   Way]    Coves]
```

### Linking Strategy

1. **Pillar → Cluster:** Every destination/activity page links back to "Kaş Turkey Guide" as authority reinforcement
2. **Cluster → Cluster:** Related articles link to each other (e.g., "Kekova" → "Patara Beach" cross-link)
3. **Informational → Commercial:** Every informational article mentions properties that match the activity (e.g., "Scuba Diving Kaş" → link to "Stay at BlueNook for easy dive access")
4. **Long-tail → Hub:** Seasonal articles and FAQs link up to main category hubs
5. **Property pages → Guides:** Each property page links to 2-3 relevant destination/activity guides

### Example Internal Links
- Kekova article: "Planning a day trip to Kekova? **[Where to Stay in Kaş]** before your boat tour" → Properties with early wake-up amenities
- Scuba Diving article: "Most dive sites are 20-40 min from **[Çukurbağ Peninsula properties]**, home to BlueNook & Villa Patara" → Specific properties
- Transport article: "**[Villa Patara]** is closest to Dalaman airport (2 hours direct)" → Transport + property cross-link

## Content Production Strategy: Multilingual (EN → TR → DE)

Each article published in English first, then:
1. **Turkish translation (Week +1):** Manual or AI-assisted, cultural localization
2. **German translation (Week +2):** Largest international market in Antalya

This 3-language rollout = 3x content production, same editorial effort.

**Example timeline:**
- Week 1 EN: "Where to Stay in Kaş" (English)
- Week 2 TR: "Kaş'ta Kalınacak Yerler" (Turkish)
- Week 3 DE: "Wo man in Kaş übernachten sollte" (German)

This ensures Turkish domestic tourists see content in their language, and German tourists (the #1 European market) get native-language guides.
